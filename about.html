About Us

We are a cozy, industrial-chic restaurant offering oysters, sushi rolls, tacos, and more. We also offer microbrews. The last three years in a row, we've been voted "Best Sushi" and "Best Asian Restaurant" in Hays by the people. We are currently in the process of moving back to Downtown Hays on Fort Street.
